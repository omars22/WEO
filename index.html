About Us

Ahmad, Haya, Sofia, Omar, and Yashaa founded the WEO (World Equality Organization) because the purpose of their IB (International Baccalaureate) school project called the Exhibition, they are interested in equality and are furious of how women and young girls are getting treated unfairly. They published WEO to the public to spread awareness worldwide to everyone of what is happening.
